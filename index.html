Humanitarian Purpose

in this website

The NEU charity fund website was established with the goal of raising financial support from the community to assist victims severely affected by Typhoon Yagi. This is an opportunity for everyone to come together and help rebuild the lives of those in need.

Connecting the community

With a user-friendly interface, the website serves as a platform connecting the NEU community, businesses, and philanthropists across the country. Users can easily track the fund’s progress, contribute, and share information with friends and family.

Transparency and Trust

of our orgazination

The website is committed to full transparency throughout the fund management process, from recording donations to distributing support to victims. Financial reports and activities will be regularly updated to ensure trustworthiness for all donors.

Our Charity Events

A lot of charity activities for everyone to participate in.

Fundraising Auction Event

Organize an auction where students, alumni, and local businesses can donate items or services for bidding. Items could include artwork created by students, memorabilia from the university, or services like tutoring or career coaching sessions.

Auctions generate excitement and allow participants to contribute more generously, especially if they are bidding on something meaningful. The competitive nature of an auction often leads to higher donations.

Charity Run

Host a sponsored run or walkathon around the university campus or a local park. Participants can gather sponsorships from friends, family, and businesses for every kilometer they complete.

You can offer prizes for the most funds raised, the best running time, or the best costume worn during the event.

Fundraising Concert

Organize a charity concert or talent show featuring performances from students, faculty members, and local artists. Charge an entry fee for tickets and accept donations during the event.

Invite guest performers or well-known alumni to boost ticket sales and excitement. Streaming the event online could also increase reach and donations.
